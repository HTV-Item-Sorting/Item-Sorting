Waste Categorization

Scan Your Item to Categorize It!

Use this tool to accurately categorize your waste items and contribute to a more sustainable environment.

↓

Categorizing your trash plays an important role in reducing our environmental impact.

By categorizing our waste properly we reduce landfill disposal, optimize resource usage, and facilitate the process of recycling.

Waste categorization is not just a responsible practice; it is a vital step towards creating a sustainable future. By making conscious decisions about how we dispose of our waste, we can contribute to a healthier planet and encourage others to do the same.

How to Use It?

1. Scroll down to access the webcam

2. Position the item in front of the camera

3. The item will be classified as Recycling, Compost, or Hazard

4. If it's Recycling, or Compost put in the respective bin, if it's Hazard don't insert it, and everything else goes in the garbage!
